Furst lucky pup: meet Skyla!


  • Name: Skyla, aka Mischief
  • Age:
  • Gender: female
  • Breed: collie/retriever cross


Today I had the pleasure of getting to know the lovely Skyla!



Skyla's a gorgeous pup, and I'm sure it's obvious why she caught my eye. Don't let the cuteness fool you, she's a cheeky devil under all that fluff! As soon as we hit the vineyard, Skyla was happy to bound through the grass and puddles. She clearly takes pleasure from the small things in life! Luckily for me, those things include a good snuggle in between play sessions.




Skyla's a sharp cookie and once she realised I had a few treats in my pocket, quickly worked out that "sit" was a good way to get them. But that was too easy for her, she quickly progress to "down" - and then "snooze"! After all the excitement of a walk and making some new friends, Skyla was content to settle down for a nap.
